Sirsasana OR Headstand pose

Sirsasana, or the Headstand pose, offers several advantages. It improves circulation to the brain, strengthens the upper body, enhances balance and stability, increases focus and concentration, provides rejuvenation and stress relief, and improves digestion and metabolism. However, it is important to practice this advanced pose under the guidance of a qualified instructor and with caution.

Dandasana OR the Staff pose

Dandasana, or the Staff pose, offers benefits such as improved posture, core engagement, increased flexibility, enhanced focus and concentration, stimulated energy flow, and preparation for other poses. Practice with awareness and within your own limits.

Virabhadrasana OR Warrior pose

Virabhadrasana, or Warrior pose, offers benefits such as increased strength, improved balance, enhanced flexibility, empowerment, and improved focus. It is a dynamic standing pose that engages the whole body.
